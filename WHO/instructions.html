Instructions

General

- Match the Figures on the bottom row with the main figure. To match, you can click or use keypresses.

- Don't underestimate the difficulty.

Keypresses

-Click F: Matches the Left figure on the bottom with the Main figure.

-Click H: Matches the Center figure on the bottom with the Main figure.

-Click G: Matches the Right figure on the bottom with the Main figure.

-Click R: Restarts your time and score. You will be using this a lot.
